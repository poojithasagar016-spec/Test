Favourite Places

Kashi

Kashi/varanasi is one of the world’s oldest cities, located on the banks of the Ganges River. It is a major spiritual center of Hinduism and is closely associated with Lord Shiva. The city is famous for the Kashi Vishwanath Temple and sacred rituals performed along its ghats.

Tiruvannamalai

Arunachalam is a sacred hill in Tiruvannamalai, associated with Lord Shiva and representing the fire element in Hindu tradition. It is famous for the Arunachaleswara Temple and the spiritual practice of Girivalam performed by devotees.

Tirupati

Tirupati is not just a place, but a deeply spiritual journey where millions come with faith, hope, and devotion in their hearts. At the sacred Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, devotees feel a profound connection with Lord Venkateswara, experiencing peace, blessings, and inner strength.
